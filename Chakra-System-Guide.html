Understanding Your Energy Centers

Your chakras are spinning wheels of energy that run along your central channel. When I perform Reiki with clients, I focus on the chakra system because it's the key to understanding what's happening in your energy field.

Each chakra should be:

  • Spinning actively
  • Open and clear
  • Its appropriate color

When chakras are imbalanced, I can see specific blockages—for example, a thread wrapped around a heart chakra might indicate a past relationship still constricting your ability to love.

Your Seven Main Chakras

Crown Chakra

Location: Top of your head

Color: Purple

Function: Your connection to divinity

When Balanced

  • You feel connected to a higher power
  • You know you're not alone
  • You experience spiritual connection

When Imbalanced

  • You feel like there is no higher power
  • You feel alone in the universe
  • You lack spiritual connection

Third Eye Chakra

Location: Brow/forehead area

Color: Deep blue or indigo

Function: Your intuition and foresight

When Balanced

  • You hear and trust your intuition
  • You have clear foresight
  • You see beyond the physical

When Imbalanced

  • You do not hear your intuition
  • You may have paranoid thoughts
  • You feel disconnected from inner knowing

Throat Chakra

Location: Throat area

Color: Blue (sky blue or turquoise)

Function: Your speech and expression

When Balanced

  • You express yourself effectively
  • You speak your truth
  • You communicate clearly

When Imbalanced

  • You feel tight in the throat area
  • You are not speaking your truth
  • You feel it is not safe to express yourself

Heart Chakra

Location: Center of your chest

Color: Green (may appear emerald)

Function: Love, being open, self-expression, acting from your heart space

When Balanced

  • You are open to love
  • You express yourself from the heart
  • You trust others appropriately

When Imbalanced

  • You feel you cannot trust other people
  • You may be living in a state of grief
  • You may feel jealous of other people
  • Past relationship energy may be constricting the chakra

Solar Plexus Chakra

Location: Absolute middle of your body, middle of your torso

Color: Yellow (like an internal sun)

Function: Self-discipline, confidence, knowing how to nourish your body appropriately

When Balanced

  • You are self-disciplined
  • You are confident
  • You know how to nourish your body appropriately

When Imbalanced

  • You may deal with disordered eating
  • You don't feel confident
  • You are probably feeling lazy

Sacral Chakra

Location: For women, in your womb space. For men, a little bit below your belly button

Color: Orange (beautiful orange)

Function: Your sexuality as a creative force

When Balanced

  • Your sexuality is a creative force
  • You have a high, balanced libido
  • You create with ease

When Imbalanced

  • You don't have libido
  • You're probably not creating anything
  • You probably feel frustrated at life in general

Root Chakra

Location: Your pelvic floor

Color: Red (beautiful red)

Function: Your safety, grounding, connection to this planet, to your body, to this reality

When Balanced

  • You feel safe
  • You feel grounded
  • You feel your needs are being met
  • You feel connected to your body and this reality

When Imbalanced

  • You probably feel unsafe
  • You feel that your needs are not being met
  • You don't feel grounded
  • You feel disconnected from your body and reality

Understanding Chakra Imbalances

When I perform Reiki on people, I can tell what imbalances are going on based on how the chakras look. I'm not necessarily digging around for personal information, but the energy shows itself.

For example, if somebody's heart chakra is imbalanced, I often will see a thread wrapped around it, and it might even be hair of a certain color. This is because maybe they had a bad breakup, and this person's hair color is constricting how they interact with love moving forward and how they love people in their life moving forward.

That's just one example. You want to have this whole system be those appropriate colors as well as spinning and open.
